The ClearPath System
Three formulas that cover your day.

ClearPath Labs will launch with a focused trio: AM, PM, and Recovery. Names and exact ingredient lists are in development, but the design philosophy is already clear: do a few things well, every single day.

1 · Morning

AM Formula

A morning formula designed to support mental clarity, motivation, and resilience—without leaving you wired at night.

  • Supports focus and task follow-through.
  • Built to coexist with coffee, not fight it.
  • Aim: clean energy, not chaos.

2 · Night

PM Formula

A pre-sleep formula focused on helping your body drop into deeper, more restorative sleep—without relying on heavy sedatives.

  • Non-habit-forming approach to sleep support.

  • Pairs with good nighttime habits.

Recovery Support

A targeted formula aimed at helping you absorb stress, training, and modern-life strain so you can show up again tomorrow.

Why ClearPath Labs
Built for people who care what’s on the label.

The supplement space doesn’t need more noise. It needs fewer, better-thought-out formulas that respect your time, your sleep, and your long-term health. That’s the lane ClearPath Labs is in.

Principle #1

Evidence and synergy first.

We start with peer-reviewed research and then look at how ingredients behave together—not just in isolation. The goal is synergy, not just a long list on the back of a bottle.

Principle #2

Transparent, grown-up labels.

No proprietary blends that hide underdosed ingredients. Clear amounts, plain language, and real reasons for why each compound is there—and what it’s not trying to do.

Principle #3

Designed for every day.

You shouldn’t need twelve products on your counter. The ClearPath system is meant to be sustainable: morning, night, and targeted recovery. Enough to make a difference; not enough to rule your life.

How It Works
From idea to bottles on your counter.

ClearPath Labs is early—but moving deliberately. If you care about how products are built, you’re in the right place.

Step 1

Research & formulation

We’re working through ingredient shortlists, dosing ranges, and compatibility—keeping an eye on AM/PM interactions so your day and night formulas don’t fight each other.

Step 2

Manufacturing partner

The next major step is locking in a manufacturing partner who can honor testing, sourcing, and batch consistency at a high level. No shortcuts, even if that slows us down.

Step 3

Testing & launch

Before broader release, early customers and friends of the brand will get first access and feedback cycles. If you’re on the list, you’ll hear about that before anyone else.

Are any products available to buy yet?
Not yet. Formulas are in development and we’re in the process of evaluating manufacturing partners. If you’re on the email list, you’ll hear about samples and launch timelines first.

Yes. The plan is full-label transparency: no proprietary blends and no hidden dosages. If something is in a formula, you’ll know exactly how much and why it’s there.
