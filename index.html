FAQ
What we can say clearly right now.

ClearPath Labs and the ClearFlow Recovery System are still in development. Here’s where things stand today.

Are these products available for purchase yet?
Not yet. Formulas, labels, and manufacturing partnerships are being finalized now. The early access list will hear about sample batches, timelines, and pricing before anything goes live publicly.
Is the ClearFlow system meant to treat post-viral syndromes?
No. ClearPath Labs products are dietary supplements, not drugs. They are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or condition. The focus is on supporting general wellness areas such as circulation, inflammatory balance, energy, and sleep.

Always talk with your healthcare provider before starting any supplement, especially if you have ongoing symptoms or take medications.
Will you publish full ingredient labels?
Yes – that’s the plan. No proprietary blends hiding dosages. If an ingredient is used, you’ll know exactly how much is in a serving and why it’s there before you ever decide to try it.
Who is the ClearFlow Recovery System for?
People who want a thoughtful, simple supplement approach to support their own work on recovery – from post-viral or chronic stress states – and who care a lot about label quality, transparency, and how things actually fit into daily life.
