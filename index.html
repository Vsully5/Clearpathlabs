Post-viral & Autonomic Nervous System
Support for a system that’s been through it.

Many people feel “off” long after a viral hit—energy swings, wired-but-tired nights, and a nervous system that feels stuck. ClearPath Labs is building formulas that aim to support the systems behind that experience, not just mask it. None of this is medical treatment—but it is designed with recovery in mind.

Post-viral reality

Energy that doesn’t bounce back.

After viral stress, it’s common to feel like your battery never fully charges. The AM and Recovery formulas are being shaped around supporting healthy mitochondrial function, stress response, and focus—so getting through the day feels a little more manageable.

  • Support for sustained daytime energy.
  • Designed to avoid “crash” patterns.
  • Built with long-term use in mind.
ANS balance

Sympathetic vs. parasympathetic.

Your autonomic nervous system (ANS) has two main modes: “go” (sympathetic) and “rest” (parasympathetic). ClearPath PM and Recovery are being designed with ingredients that may support calm, relaxation, and healthy stress signaling—so your system can better find neutral again.

  • Support for downshifting at night.
  • Help for feeling less “stuck on.”
  • Focused on balance, not sedation.
Whole-day support

Morning, night, and the space between.

The stack is meant to follow your actual day: how you wake, how you perform, and how you come down. Instead of relying on one do-it-all product, ClearPath splits support into focused formulas that can be tuned and refined over time.

  • AM: support for clarity and drive.
  • PM: support for deeper rest.
  • Recovery: support for resilience.

Questions
What we can say clearly right now.

ClearPath Labs is early—but serious. Here are a few straight answers based on where things are today.

Are any products available for purchase yet?
Not yet. Formulas, label copy, and manufacturing partnerships are being developed now. The early access list will hear about samples, timelines, and pricing before anything goes public.
Is this meant to treat post-viral syndromes?
No. ClearPath Labs products are dietary supplements, not drugs. They are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or condition. The focus is on supporting systems involved in energy, sleep, and stress response in a general wellness context.

Always talk to your healthcare provider before starting any supplement, especially if you have ongoing symptoms.
Will you publish full ingredient labels?
Yes. The plan is full-label transparency—no proprietary blends hiding dosages. If something is in a formula, you’ll know how much and why it’s there before you ever decide to take it.
Who is this stack really for?
People who want a calmer, clearer, more resilient baseline—especially if they’ve been through viral stress or long stretches of overload and want to be thoughtful about what they put in their body.
