FAQ

Questions people ask most often.

How long does it take to feel a difference?
Everyone’s timeline is different, especially after viral or chronic stress. Some people notice changes in circulation, clarity, or sleep in the first few weeks; for others it’s slower and more gradual. ClearFlow is designed for consistent daily use, not an instant quick-fix.
Can I take the bottles separately?
Yes. Each formula can be used on its own, but the system was built so AM, Day, and PM support different phases that work together. Your practitioner can help you decide which phases to start with.
Is this safe with my medications or diagnosis?
Because everyone’s health history is unique, always review the full ingredient list with your licensed healthcare provider or pharmacist before starting any new supplement.
